About Reyna Villanueva

Born in Cebu, Philippines, Reyna Villanueva discovered her passion for photography at the age of fifteen, inspired by the vibrant culture, colorful festivals, and rich traditions of her homeland. Her early works merged self-portraiture with conceptual art, reflecting a deep connection to her roots while exploring new ways to express personal identity through the lens. After studying Visual Arts, Reyna moved to London to immerse herself in its diverse creative community and expand her artistic vision within the global fashion industry.

Reyna specializes in conceptual fashion and beauty photography, blending surrealism with high fashion to create striking, narrative-driven images. Drawing from her Filipino heritage, she incorporates traditional textures, colors, and storytelling into her work, while challenging conventional beauty standards and redefining fashion photography. Now based in London, Reyna collaborates with independent fashion brands and creatives from around the world. Her work is often centered on sustainability, with a focus on upcycling and incorporating vintage elements into her shoots. Passionate about nurturing the next generation of photographers, Reyna also teaches and mentors emerging creatives, helping them find their unique voice and develop their artistic vision.
